📸 The Realisation

💌 When Love Chose you

I don’t know why —but that day, I realized I was in love. At first, it was just a feeling I couldn’t explain. But slowly, it grew into something deeper, something beautiful. Day by day, you became the most important part of my life.
you became the one I thought about the most, cared for the most, and loved more than anything.
And today, I can’t imagine a life without you in it.

📸 Stalking

💌 A Little Closer Each Day

Little by little, I got more attached. I started checking your Instagram quietly—scrolling through old stories, rewatching them , just to see your smile again. I don’t know why, but it made me feel close to you, even if you had no idea. I noticed everything—the reels you liked, your friends, even your family. It wasn’t just interest anymore… it was something deeper. Something I didn’t have words for, but felt every single day.

📸 Love you

💌 The Song You Didn’t Know You Left Behind

One day, I was playing this song. You said that you also like this song, That was it. Nothing dramatic. Just a moment. But it stayed with me. I started listening to it more… and more. Eventually, it became my top song on Spotify. Not because it was your favorite. Not even because of the song itself. But because, for a second, you and I shared that silence — together. Now, every time it plays, it takes me back to that quiet, beautiful seconds.
you probably forgot… but I never will.

📸 I never wanted to lose you

💌 The Fear Behind the Feeling

I sometimes look back at those days… Back when I was scared to lose you. I remember holding back, Not because I didn’t feel something deep for you… But because I felt too much. Loving you was beautiful even then. But the fear of losing you? That was overwhelming. And now, having you beside me, I realize how worth it every silent moment, every held-back word was. You were always the one I never wanted to lose — and now… I don’t have to.

📸 A Moment of Trust

💌 Because She Felt Like a Sister

I really thought I was hiding it well. The way I looked at you, the quiet feelings building up inside me. But that day, Swathi looked at me and asked, "Do you like her?" — For a moment, I didn’t know what to say. But with Swathi, it was different. She felt like a sister — someone who just understood me without needing too many words. That was the first time someone saw through me. And it felt both scary… and safe. After a pause, I quietly said, "Yeah… I do."

I used to whisper “sorry” in my mind — for feeling more than I should’ve. Sometimes I’d even type it out, then delete it before you saw. I worried if I was making things harder for you. I loved you quietly, afraid to cross a line. But now… I don’t have to hide it anymore. Now I can love you out loud.

💌 That One Hold

Before our class trip to Kakkadampoyil, I found out—through Swathi—that you was going to wear black. I didn’t own a black shirt, but it felt important. I sat behind you, heart racing, trying to gather courage to confess. But the words never came.
